Economics Literacy Quiz

“How Literate -- Economically -- are businesspeople?” We thought to test our readers with a short quiz and hope you do as well!

--From "Across the Board", December 2012

Directions: There are 10 questions and each has four possible answers. Select an answer and press the submit button, then an a pop-up box will tell you if you are correct. Press 'ok' to advance to the next question.

Your running score will be displayed as you progress, and, at the end, you can choose to review the questions or play again. You are awesome.
